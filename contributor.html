Code of conduct
1
Be honest. Only review places you've actually been. Write what you actually think. No fake reviews, no paid posts.
2
Be specific. "Good pizza" tells nobody anything. Describe the crust, the char, what to order. Give someone a real reason to go — or skip it.
3
Be fair. A critical review is fine. A cruel one isn't. Criticize the pizza, not the person who made it.
4
Your reviews stay yours. Pizza Official indexes your reviews — it doesn't own them. Delete your account any time and your data goes with it.
5
Don't be a jerk. Treat everyone with respect.
